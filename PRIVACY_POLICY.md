# Privacy Policy — Boero Camera

_Last updated: March 2026_

Boero Camera is a camera application developed by Johnny Boero. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

Boero Camera does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising SDKs, and no network connectivity of any kind.

## Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions, all of which are used exclusively on-device:

- **Camera** — to capture photos and video.
- **Microphone** — to record audio with video.
- **Storage / Media** — to save photos and videos to your device.

None of this data leaves your device.

## Photos and Videos

All photos and videos are saved directly to your device's local storage. They are never uploaded, transmitted, or accessed by anyone other than you.

## Third Parties

Boero Camera does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect data.

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
